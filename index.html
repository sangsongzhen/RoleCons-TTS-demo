语音合成 Demo

下表展示了 Ground-Truth、DailyTalk、Proposed Model 三种系统在不同对话下的生成效果。

对话文本 Ground-Truth DailyTalk Proposed Model
Speaker 1: I am looking for a pan.
Speaker 0: No problem. What size would you like?
Speaker 1: A big one would be nice.
Speaker 0: How about this one? It's our biggest — sixteen' in diameter.
Speaker 1: Oh, yes, I like that one, but it's too heavy.
Speaker 0: OK, try this one. It's made of aluminum.
Speaker 1: Oh, yes! This is much better. But it has an aluminum handle.
Speaker 0: Here you go. Same pan, but with a state-of-the-art, heat-resistant plastic handle.
Speaker 1: Yes. That's perfect. I'll take it.
Speaker 0: Great. Will that be cash or credit?
Speaker 1: Oh, wait a minute. What about a lid for the pan?
Speaker 0: I'm sorry. I forgot to show you the lid. It comes with the pan.
